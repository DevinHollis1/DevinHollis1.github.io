About Me!

Hello! My name is Devin Hollis, and this is me. When i was younger i dreamed of being an artist, i would sit in my room and draw for hours, not knowing the time of day half the time. all of that creativity, and imagination never stopped and is still with me in whatever i do.

Making Music

I have been making music for as long as I can remember. This skill involves a deep understanding of musical elements and the ability to weave them together seamlessly. It requires a sharp ear for capturing intricate nuances and utilizing digital tools. I have over 10,000 combined beats, melodies, and songs that I worked on or did myself! Whether it's composing original pieces or arranging existing music, the skill of making music empowers me to be creative and brings joy to me and my listeners.
